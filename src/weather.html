Weather In Vietnam

In Hanoi

  • December - March: cool to cold, misty, cloudy
  • April - June: warm to hot, clear skies, sunny
  • June - August: hot, humid, rainy
  • September - November: cool, clear skies, sunny

The best time to visit Hanoi is usually from April to June or from September to December. From April the days are normally hot, occasionally above 30 degrees with clear skies. September to November sees cooler temperatures of around 25 degrees and sunny skies -- perfect for exploring the capital.

If you’re coming to Hanoi in June, expect soaring temperatures with humidity highs of 40 degrees, and the rainy season is full swing (June to August). December is cool, misty and atmospheric, however from late December until March temperatures are surprisingly cold with lows of 10 degrees. You’ll want to bundle up.

In Hoi An

  • September - January: rainy, cool, cloudy
  • February - August: warm to hot, sunny, clear skies

Hoi An is one of the country’s picture-perfect places, but starting in September, tropical storms hit Central Vietnam hard. March is an excellent time to see Hội An when the fields, gardens and streets are awash in bright blossoms and soothing greens. From May to August, the town can be hot, and you’ll want to head to the beach to cool off. In the late months of the year, from September to November, Hội An sees a lot of rain and even a few small floods. The temperatures begin to dip and reach their coldest point in January, warming up just before the Vietnamese new year.

In Ho Chi Minh

  • May to November: sporadic rainfall, hot, humid
  • December to April: clear skies, hot, sunny

Ho Chi Minh city have consistent temperatures year-round. HCMC is either hot and dry or hot and rainy, although there are some pleasant fresh days early in the year, and some scorching days mid-year. Temperatures are normally around 30 degrees, with sunshine. From May to November, the wet season brings a dependable afternoon downpour, usually lasting a few hours.

In Ha Giang

  • April - September: warm to hot, sunny, clear skies
  • October - March: cold, rainy, cloudy.

The average temperature in Ha Giangranges from 15°C to 28°C, depending on the season. The summer months from June to August are the hottest, with temperatures ranging from 24°C to 28°C, and high humidity levels that can make it feel even hotter. During this time, frequent rain showers and occasional thunderstorms can also be expected.

The winter months from December to February are the coldest, with temperatures ranging from 5°C to 16°C, and occasional frost or snow in the highlands. Visitors should be prepared for colder temperatures, especially at night, and pack warm clothing and blankets.

The best time to visit Ha Giang in terms of weather is during the autumn months from September to November. During this time, the weather is cooler and drier, with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 24°C. The skies are clear, and visitors can enjoy spectacular views of the rice terraces and mountain landscapes.

In Ha Long

  • December - March: cool to cold, misty, cloudy
  • April - June: warm to hot, clear skies, sunny
  • June - August: hot, humid, stormy
  • September - November: warm to cool, mix of sun and clouds

If you’re coming to Ha Long Bay, try to visit from April to June or September to November. You’ll have a better chance of enjoying sunny skies and pleasant temperatures. Other times of year, downpour or heavy mist is common, and tropical storms can sometimes cause cruise cancellations. However, if you’re on the water during this time you can experience a more mystical side of Halong Bay, with its famous karsts shrouded in mist. Temperatures in January can be frigid, so be sure to bring warm layers.
